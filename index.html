About the Course

Learn PM by building.

This course is built on a simple belief: you don't learn product management by reading about it—you learn it by building. We start with the fundamentals—product thinking in the age of AI. You'll learn how to use tools like Claude Code to generate PRDs, accelerate prototyping, and move from idea to execution faster than ever. And throughout every module, you'll have access to private 1:1 coaching sessions dedicated to your product, your challenges, and your goals.

Throughout the entire course, AI-driven prototyping is not a module—it's the foundation. Private coaching isn't office hours—it's dedicated sessions on your actual work. This course is for builders. For operators. For product thinkers who want to move faster and go deeper.
